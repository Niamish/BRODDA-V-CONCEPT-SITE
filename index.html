ABOUT THE ARTIST

WHERE MYTHOLOGY MEETS THE STREET

Vighnesh Shivanand, known by his stage name Brodha V, is an Indian rapper and music producer from Bangalore. A pioneer of the Indian hip-hop scene, he seamlessly blends ancient Indian mythology with modern street culture.

With razor-sharp lyrics delivered in English, Hindi, and Kannada, Brodha V has carved a unique niche in the industry, creating anthems that resonate with the youth while paying homage to cultural roots.

From underground battles to mainstream recognition, his journey represents the evolution of Indian hip-hop itself.
